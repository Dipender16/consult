How Does Australia Work Permit Application Process Works?

Foreign nationals who are travelling to Australia to work need to have a National Long-Stay Visa, or D Visa.

The D visa allows foreign nationals to stay in Australia for more than three months. This is the visa foreign employees will need to live and work in Australia for an extended period of time.

In addition to a long-stay visa, foreign employees will need a work permit. In Australia, this permit is known as an employment license. The employment license is usually issued for 1 year.

What Documents Are Required To Acquire Australia Work Employment License/Work Permit?

  • A valid passport
  • Travel medical insurance
  • An employment contract with a Australia-based company.
  • Proof of accommodations for the duration of the applicant’s time in Australia
  • Proof of sufficient financial means to cover the entire period of the applicant’s stay
  • Relevant Work Experience
  • Updated Resume
  • Candidates should be currently working

    What is the Process to File for Australia Visa?

  • Completed Visa Application Form
  • One recent passport-sized colour photo with white background in electronic format.
  • Copy of the Valid Travel Document, the period of validity of which should exceed that of the visa by three months.
  • Confirmed Flight Booking (stamped and signed by travel agent) or copy of the actual flight ticket showing the full itinerary of your trip.
  • Confirmed Hotel Reservation.
  • Health Insurance acceptable in the Schengen Area (minimum coverage has to be 30,000 Euros) and should cover the period of validity of the visa.
